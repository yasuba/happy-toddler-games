## Notes

- "Cannot be redistributed standalone" clauses (as on the plop sound)
  do not affect use within an app — embedding in a game constitutes
  sufficient creative transformation.
- If submitting to the App Store, this file serves as your licence
  paper trail. Keep download dates and URLs accurate.
- Check each source for any attribution requirements before shipping.
  freetouse.com tracks do not require attribution but it is good practice.
